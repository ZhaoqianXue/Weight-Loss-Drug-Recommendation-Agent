For 31 years, I attempted to lower my LDL cholesterol, but despite my physical achievements in the U.S. Army, my levels remained high, between 225-300. After trying every statin available through the VA with adverse effects like muscle atrophy, I began using Alirocumab (Praluent) three months ago, administering it every two weeks. My cholesterol has dropped to 147 with zero side effects, except for one the manufacturer's claim doesn't occur: weight loss. I started at 214 lbs and now weigh 197.8 ... lbs, my military weight. My exercise routine is minimal, and my diet has changed slightly, with less processed food. Aside from triglycerides, recent lab results returned exceptionally normal compared to past metrics.
